WILBUR'S IDEAS FOR YOUNG PEOPLE TO HAVE FUN WITHOUT CLEANING HIS DATA

1 Invent a musical instrument.
2 Learn a language.
3 Engage in risky behaviours.
3.1 Hitch-hiking.
3.2 Mushroom-foraging.
4 Learn mathematics.
5 Talk to strangers.
5.1 On a bus.
5.2 At a bar. (When I was 17 I had two strategies to avoid being carded: (a) find a dirty beer glass and ask "for a refill"; (b) adopt a strong, generically-European accent. ymmv.)
6 Read the manifestos of long-defunct philosophical movements.
7 Organize politically.
7.1 Nothing racist please.
